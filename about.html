About Me

Hi, I'm Finley. I'm from Chicago and I'm currently an intern at Eight Bit Studios. I draw stuff on occasion and I design stuff, like this website. I write blog posts and science fiction. I'm a rising senior in high school. Which high school I go to is entirely up to your imagination. I have a questionable fashion sense, as well as a questionable sense in pets. I have a lizard named Dipper who watches me while I work and judges silently.

I've had an interest in graphic design since eighth grade, but it never overshadowed my passion for illustration. That's why I try to integrate it into my design work. Of course, I still draw on the side, as well. You can see all that under "My Work".
